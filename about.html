Flame Fusion was created with a focus on enthusiasts of fire. Both our customers and employees alike are united by a shared enthusiasm for community and the warmth of fires that unite us.

Our Story

Similar to numerous captivating tales, our journey commenced with three siblings, a sky adorned with stars, and a crackling campfire. Meet, Yash, and Zameer collectively envisioned an improved outdoor fire pit tailored for the Canadian lifestyle. Consequently, they manually crafted their inaugural fire pit, a creation that still serves the family to this very day!

2015

During the summer of 2015, the trio officially founded Flame Fusion with the aspiration of spreading their fervor to a global audience. Commencing operations from their garage, they initially crafted robust fire pits. Swiftly, their creations found a market at local farmers'markets. The turning point arrived when they secured their inaugural distributor that very summer, marking the beginning of an unstoppable journey forward.

2019

Flame Fusion joyously commemorated numerous significant milestones. The year 2019 symbolized a decade of crafting Canada's premier fire pits, coinciding with our relocation to a spacious new workshop in Guelph, Ontario. Undeterred by the passage of time, our dedicated team persists in spreading our passion to customers throughout Canada and the United States, now operating from our expanded and upgraded headquarters.

Present

From its humble beginnings as a familial venture, Flame Fusion has evolved into a trailblazer within the premium outdoor fire pit community. Each year, thousands of fervent fire aficionados join our community, serving as a wellspring of inspiration propelling us to consistently push boundaries. Our dedication remains unwavering as we actively pursue the introduction of groundbreaking products, firmly asserting that there are no intentions of slowing down.
