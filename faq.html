Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I choose the right fire pit for my backyard?

A: Consider the size of your space, the type of fuel you prefer (wood, gas, or propane), and any safety features you may need. Our experts can also help you make the right choice.

Q: Can I use a gas fire pit on my wooden deck?

A: Yes, you can use a gas fire pit on a wooden deck, but it's essential to follow safety guidelines. Make sure there is proper ventilation, and consider using a heat-resistant barrier underneath the fire pit.

Q: Do you offer installation services?

A: Yes, we provide professional installation services for our fire pits. Contact our team to schedule an installation appointment.

Q: What maintenance is required for a fire pit?

A: Regular maintenance includes cleaning the fire pit, checking gas connections (if applicable), and inspecting for any signs of wear. Refer to the user manual for specific care instructions.

Q: Can I roast marshmallows over a gas fire pit?

A: Yes, you can roast marshmallows over a gas fire pit, but make sure it's designed for such use. Use skewers with a safe distance from the flame, and always supervise children around the fire pit.

Q: Are your fire pits weather-resistant?

A: Our fire pits are built to withstand various weather conditions. However, for prolonged durability, we recommend using a cover when not in use and storing it indoors during extreme weather.
